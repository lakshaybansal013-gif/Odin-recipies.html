Pizza

Description

This homemade pizza recipe uses a simple dough made from flour, yeast, salt, sugar, water, and olive oil, kneaded until smooth and rested for 1-5 hours. Shape the dough, add pizza sauce, mozzarella, and toppings, then bake at high heat (425–500°F/220–260°C) for 10-15 minutes until golden and bubbly.

Ingredients

  • Dough: 2 ½ cups (300g) all-purpose flour or bread flour, 1 teaspoon instant yeast, 1 teaspoon salt, 1 teaspoon sugar, 1 tablespoon olive oil, 1 cup warm water.
  • Toppings: 1/2 cup pizza sauce, 1-2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, toppings of choice (pepperoni, vegetables).

Steps

  1. Prepare the Dough: Mix flour, yeast, salt, and sugar. Add warm water and olive oil, stirring until a dough forms.
  2. Knead and Rise: Knead for 5–10 minutes until smooth and elastic. Place in an oiled bowl, cover, and let it rise in a warm place for 1–5 hours until doubled in size.
  3. Shape: Divide the dough. On a floured surface, stretch or roll the dough into a 10–12 inch circle.
  4. Top: Spread sauce, add cheese, and add toppings.
  5. Bake: Bake at 425–450°F on a preheated pizza pan or stone for 10–15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
